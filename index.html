    About Us

    Kottayam is known as the land of letters, the first town of India to attain 100% adult literacy. It is 146 kilometers away from the State Capital, Thiruvananthapuram, and bordered by Districts Pathanamthitta on the South, Alapuzha on the West, Ernakulam on the North, and Idukki on the East. Valavoor is Six kilometers from Pala which is the main Gateway to the Southern High ranges of the Western ghats. The prestigious tourist locationVagamon is 45 kilometers closed to Pala while Elavizhapoonchira is about 25 kilometers from Pala. Pala is the transit point to various tourism locations such as Periyar Wild Sanctuary, Thekkady, Rajamala, Moonnar, Thattekad Wild Sanctuary, and Gavi. Vembanad Lake and Kumarakom, the world famous tourist destinations are 35 kilometers away from Valavoor. The Siva Temple Kadappatoor, Pala, St. AlphonsaShrine, Bharananganam, Nalambalam Temples, Ramapuram are the famous pilgrim centers around Valavoor and Pala.

    Kerala State Co-operative Tyre Factory Ltd. No. 4398, Kottayam was promoted for establishing a Radial Tyre Project with a Capital outlay of 600 Crores, through value addition of Natural Rubber produced by the small and marginal farmers. Since the Society could not mobilize the required capital for establishing the project and also certain reasons beyond the control of the Board of Directors, the radial tyre project was abandoned. Efforts were also made for a joint venture with M/s. Trelleborg Lanka (P) Ltd. for manufacturing solid tyres in the year 2006-07. Accordingly, a Technically Specified Block Rubber (TSR) manufacturing unit using natural rubber latex was proposed. However, due to the public resistance/litigation against the project on the ground of pollution, Society was forced to give up the project. In the year 2014, State Government acquired about 55 acres of land in the locality for establishing an Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT-K) complex out of which 12 acres of land belonging to the Society was also acquired for the IIIT Project. The Registered Office of the Kerala State Co-operative Tyre Factory Ltd., No.4398 is located at Valavoor near Pala, Kottayam District.

    Plan




    The Indian Institute of Information Technology, Kottayam [IIIT -(K)] established at Valavoor, is a Joint Venture project by the State Government and the Government of India with public-private participation. It is an autonomous residential Educational Institute, first in South India, and has an investment of Rs.300 Crores by the Central and State. The number of people coming into this IIIT Campus and future growth of internal/domestic and international tourism, M/s. PalazhyAgro Farms And Tourism Ltd.( A Company promoted by the Society) is establishing a Farm Tourism project, adjacent to the Campus in an area of about 5 acres of land, by constructing Farm Cottages. The project will be completed by March 2022

    The business and trading opportunities at Valavoor are fast-growing with the establishment of IIIT – (K) (The Indian Institute of Information Technology, Kottayam) by the Government. Considering the fast-developing scenario of the area with the establishment of IIIT complex, the Board of Directors of the Society, decided to construct an Office Building Cum Shopping Complex in an area of 50 cents owned by the Society, at Valavoor in MeenachilTaluk, Kottayam Dist. The land is adjacent to Valavoor –IIIT Chakkampuzha PWD Road, close to the IIIT Campus, and about 50 meters away from the main gate of the IIIT – (K). On successful completion of the project, the Society will be shifting the Regd. Office from rented building to own premises and can use the components for commercial activities.

    The academic and business activities in the Campus and Valavoor are increasing every year. Since the floating population visiting the area is also growing per the Campus activity that leads to an increased requirement of banking infrastructure facilities. Taking this into account, the Society intends to provide space for a Banking Extension Counter with an ATM facility, Mini Super Market, Restaurant with Bakery and Cold Storage, etc. in the proposed project for the benefit of all types of customers. The students, parents and the public who come over to the campus and Valavoor can make use of this facility.
